Tips to Stay Safe Online

Use Strong Passwords

Mix letters, numbers & symbols. Avoid names or birthdays.

Double-check Links

Fake URLs often look like real ones. Hover before clicking.

Update Regularly

Outdated systems are easy targets. Keep everything up-to-date.

Beware of UPI Frauds

Never share OTPs. Banks never ask for passwords.
