Essential Safety Practices

  • Use Strong Passwords: Create unique passwords with letters, numbers & symbols. Never reuse passwords.
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Add an extra layer of security for your accounts.
  • Beware of Phishing: Never click suspicious links or download unknown attachments.
  • Update Software Regularly: Always keep your operating system, apps, and antivirus up-to-date.
  • Check URLs Carefully: Fake websites often mimic real ones. Look for spelling errors and use HTTPS.
  • Protect Personal Information: Donâ€™t overshare on social media or unknown forms.
  • Secure Your Devices: Use screen locks and encryption on phones and computers.
  • Be Cautious with Public Wi-Fi: Avoid accessing sensitive info on open networks.
  • Regularly Backup Important Data: Use cloud or external drives to keep backups.
  • Report Suspicious Activity: If you notice anything unusual, report to relevant authorities or your bank.
